# Juggling Workshop: Nordic Tour

Recap of the juggling workshops conducted across major Nordic cities during the winter tour.

## Tour Schedule

The Nordic tour included workshops in Stockholm, Oslo, Copenhagen, and Helsinki, each tailored to the local juggling communities.

## Workshop Content

Each workshop covered basic to advanced techniques, with special focus on cold-weather adaptations for street performers in Nordic climates.

## Community Response

The tour connected jugglers across the Nordic region and established ongoing collaboration between previously isolated juggling communities.
