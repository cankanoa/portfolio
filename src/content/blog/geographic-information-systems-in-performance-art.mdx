# Geographic Information Systems in Performance Art

How GIS can be used to analyze and optimize performance art installations and events.

## Introduction to GIS in Art

Geographic Information Systems offer powerful tools for planning and analyzing performance art, especially for large-scale or site-specific installations.

## Spatial Analysis Techniques

Learn how to use spatial analysis to identify optimal locations, manage audience flow, and create immersive experiences that respond to geographic context.

## Digital Mapping Tools

Modern GIS tools allow artists to visualize and plan their performances with precision, accounting for environmental factors and audience interaction patterns.
