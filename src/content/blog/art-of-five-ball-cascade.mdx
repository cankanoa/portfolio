# The Art of Five Ball Cascade

A step-by-step guide to mastering the five ball cascade, one of juggling's classic patterns.

## Getting Started

The five ball cascade is considered one of the fundamental milestones in a juggler's journey. This pattern requires precision, rhythm, and consistent practice.

## Training Techniques

Start with three balls to build muscle memory, then progress to four, and finally five. Focus on consistent throws and hand positioning before attempting to run the full pattern.

## Common Mistakes

Many beginners throw too far forward or don't maintain a consistent rhythm. Focus on keeping your throws at a consistent height and pace.
